Student Services Centre

The Primary objective of the Student Service Centre, located on Level 2, is to provide comprehensive support in matters pertaining to students’ academic pursuits at the University such as:
  • Admissions advice
  • Basic IT support
  • Enrolment advice
  • Fees advice and information
  • Applying for adverse circumstance
  • Official transcripts
  • Parking permits
  • Degree advice and information
  • Student ID cards
  • Travel concession information (OPAL)
  • Immigration and Visa Concerns
  • Academic, Personal and Social Issues.
