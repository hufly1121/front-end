Orientation

welcome message

Welcome to your University! University of Newcastle is thrilled to have you join our vibrant and diverse community. It is filled with learners, innovators, and scholars. Congratulations to you on choosing this University to embark on your educational journey
At University of Newcastle, we are committed to providing you with abundant opportunities for your personal as well as academic growth. The university has an exceptional academic environment and a supportive community that will challenge and inspire you to achieve new heights. Throughout your time here, you will have the opportunity to learn from the best faculty members who are passionate about teaching and research. They will help you in acquiring knowledge, develop critical thinking skills and help you with practical experiences that will prepare you for success wherever you choose to go in life.
Your time here should not be just about earning a degree. You should consider it as an opportunity to discover your passion, develop skills and nurture your talent and try to make a positive impact on the world. At University of Newcastle we value diverse cultures, perspective, and ideas. We are here to create an enriching and welcoming environment for everyone.

PLAN YOUR ORIENTATION

Welcome to your Orientation week! University of Newcastle has designed a day for students to familiarise themselves to the university campus, academic programs, support services and overall life at Uni. The list of activities and timings are same for every university location, please check below for the date of the orientation. that will happen on your orientation day are as follows:
  • Welcome speech
    Time: 09:00 am
    Location: the great hall
    A formal welcome speech by the University administrator
  • Introduction to Course coordinators
    Time: 10:00 am onwards
    Location: Displayed according to degrees at the great hall
  • Campus tours
    Time 11:00 am onwards
    Location: Displayed at the great hall
  • Presentations and workshops
    Time: 12 pm onwards
    Location: Maths building
  • Lunch and free goodies
    Time: 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm
    Location: Outside great hall
  • Activities
    Time: All day
    Location: Outside great hall
